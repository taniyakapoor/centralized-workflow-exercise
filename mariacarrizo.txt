Recipe for Quesillo:

Ingredients:
- 6 eggs
- 1 can of condensed milk
- 1 cup of milk
- 6 tbsp of water
- 1/4 cup of sugar
- vanilla essence

Instructions:
- Add the eggs, condensed milk, milk and vanilla essence to the blender
- Blend for 4 minutes
- In a aluminum pot heat and stir softly the water and sugar until caramel color
- Spread the caramel in the bottom and walls of the pot
- Put the blended mix in the pot gently
- Cover the aluminum pot with tin foil
- Put the pot for 1 hr in the oven at 180 centigrades
